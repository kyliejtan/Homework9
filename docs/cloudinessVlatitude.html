Cloudiness

In regard to the relationship we observed between latitude and cloudiness, the equatorial and sub-equatorial regions of the world appear to be cloudier than other regions of the world. This is consistent with these regions being generally wetter that other regions of the world and therefore being able to fuel more evaporation and evapotransiration to the atmosphere which will eventually result in precipitation in the form of clouds.
